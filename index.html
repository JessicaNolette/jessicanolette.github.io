Who I am

I'm a developer and entrepreneur living in Maine.

Ambitious, self-driven, eager to learn, and ready for a career change. Over the past 6 months, I have demonstrated that I am disciplined and self-motivated by dedicating 40+ hours per week to expand my existing foundation in web development, computer science, and software engineering.

In addition to owning and operating Flask Lounge for 13 years, I was inspired to create a web application (Mindbosa) while training for the Chicago Marathon. During my training, I saved $1 per mile and used the money to fund my trip to Chicago. I successfully designed and worked with experienced developers in deploying Mindbosa to help others save money through goal-oriented earning. Since launching in 2017, Mindbosa has helped users save $40,000.

Coding energizes me, and I am passionate about collaborating with a team of experts to help build successful projects for wide-ranging clients.
